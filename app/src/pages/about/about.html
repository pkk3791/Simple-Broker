About HybridOSN

HOSN is an open source app that allows you to use Twitter with additional means of PRIVACY control. Besides using Twitter normally, HOSN provides you with the ability to tweet privately and like tweets anonymously. Your private actions are encrypted and securely stored in a private network, which is accessible only to your followers away from the commercial service providers.
